Home · Services

Design, build & maintain

Six disciplines, one studio — everything your landscape needs, coordinated by a single accountable team.

01

Landscape Design

Every project begins on paper. We study your home, your site and how you want to use it, then compose a master plan that ties the whole property together.

  • Master planning & site analysis
  • Planting design & material palettes
  • 3D concepts so you can picture the result
02

Hardscape & Stonework

The architecture of the landscape. We build terraces, walkways and walls with proper bases and quality stone — surfaces meant to look as good in ten years as on day one.

  • Patios, terraces & walkways
  • Retaining & seat walls in natural stone or block
  • Paver & natural-stone driveways
03

Outdoor Living

Outdoor rooms designed for real life — cooking, gathering and unwinding. We integrate kitchens, fire and shade so the spaces feel like a natural extension of the home.

  • Outdoor kitchens & dining terraces
  • Fireplaces, fire pits & water features
  • Pergolas, pavilions & arbors
04

Plantings & Gardens

The living layer that softens and finishes everything. We select trees, shrubs and color suited to Alabama's climate and your light — and plant them to establish well.

  • Trees, shrubs & foundation plantings
  • Seasonal color & garden beds
  • Fresh sod, soil prep & bed edging
05

Lighting & Irrigation

The systems that protect and extend your landscape. Architectural lighting brings it to life after dark; smart irrigation and drainage keep it healthy and dry where it should be.

  • Low-voltage landscape & architectural lighting
  • Smart irrigation systems
  • Drainage & grading solutions
06

Estate Maintenance

A beautiful landscape stays beautiful with care. Our maintenance programs handle the pruning, color changes and seasonal work that keep a finished property pristine.

  • Pruning, bed care & fertilization
  • Seasonal color rotations & cleanups
  • Tailored programs for any property size
