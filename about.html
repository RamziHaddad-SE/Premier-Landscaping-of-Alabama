Who We Are

Rooted in Birmingham, built on craft

Premier Landscaping of Alabama brings design, construction and care together under one roof. We work across Birmingham and Shelby County, partnering with homeowners who want more than a tidy yard — they want an outdoor space that reflects the quality of the home it surrounds.

Our work spans the full arc of a landscape: a thoughtful plan, expert hardscape and planting, and the ongoing care that lets it mature gracefully. Because the same team sees a project from sketch to upkeep, nothing falls through the cracks.

The result is landscapes that feel inevitable — as if they could never have been any other way.

What Guides Us

We design for how you'll actually live outdoors — then build it to outlast the trend that inspired it.
